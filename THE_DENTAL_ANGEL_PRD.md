## Product Requirements Document (PRD)
### Version 1.0 | Genius Level | Generated January 2026

# 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**The Dental Angel** is a mobile application that helps patients understand dental treatment plans before making expensive decisions. Powered by 40 years of real clinical experience and delivered through "Dr. Angel" - a warm, AI-generated dentist persona - the app transforms confusing dental jargon into plain-English explanations patients can trust.

**Mission**: Empower every patient to make informed dental decisions with confidence.

**Tagline**: *"Understand before you decide."*

**Key Metrics (Target Year 1)**:
- 100,000 downloads
- 10,000 paying subscribers ($1.2M ARR)
- 50 dental practice partners
- 4.8+ App Store rating

**PMF Score**: 8.9/10 (validated)

---

# 2. PROBLEM STATEMENT

## 2.1 The Pain Point

Every year, millions of patients leave dental offices confused and uncertain about treatment plans costing $1,000 to $50,000+. They face a critical decision with incomplete understanding:

| Pain Point | Impact |
|------------|--------|
| **Can't understand terminology** | "What's a crown vs. a cap? What's #14?" |
| **Don't know if it's necessary** | "Do I really need this, or are they upselling?" |
| **Can't explain to family** | "My spouse wants to know why this costs $8,000" |
| **Feel rushed to decide** | "They want an answer by Friday" |
| **No trusted second resource** | "Google gives me horror stories" |

## 2.2 Quantified Impact

- **150 million** dental visits per year in the US alone
- **$140 billion** spent on dental services annually (US)
- **47%** of patients delay or avoid dental treatment due to confusion/fear
- **Average treatment plan confusion**: Patient understands only 23% of what was explained (ADA study)
- **Cost of delayed treatment**: 3-5x higher when problems compound

## 2.3 Current Solutions (And Why They Fail)

| Solution | Problem |
|----------|---------|
| **Google/WebMD** | Generic, scary, not personalized, no context |
| **Ask the dentist** | Rushed, jargon-heavy, conflict of interest perception |
| **Ask friends/family** | Anecdotal, often wrong, increases anxiety |
| **Second opinion (another dentist)** | Expensive ($50-200), time-consuming, awkward |
| **Dental forums** | Unverified, inconsistent quality, anxiety-inducing |

## 2.4 Competitive Landscape Matrix

| Competitor | Personalized | Trustworthy | Available 24/7 | Plain Language | Community | Cost |
|------------|--------------|-------------|----------------|----------------|-----------|------|
| Google | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | Free |
| WebMD | ❌ | ⚠️ | ✅ | ⚠️ | ❌ | Free |
| Second Opinion | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ⚠️ | ❌ | $50-200 |
| Dental Forums | ⚠️ | ❌ | ✅ | ⚠️ | ✅ | Free |
| **The Dental Angel** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | $0-9.99/mo |

# 5. DETAILED FEATURE SPECIFICATION

## 5.1 MVP Scope (Must Have - Phase 1)

### F1: AI Chat with Dr. Angel
**Description**: Conversational AI assistant that answers dental questions in plain English with the personality of a warm, experienced dentist.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- [ ] User can type or speak questions
- [ ] Responses use plain language (8th grade reading level)
- [ ] Dr. Angel personality consistent across all responses
- [ ] Legal disclaimers included appropriately
- [ ] Response time < 3 seconds
- [ ] Conversation history preserved within session

**Story Points**: 13

---

### F2: Treatment Plan Photo Upload
**Description**: User can photograph their treatment plan and get instant explanations.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- [ ] Camera access with clear instructions
- [ ] OCR extracts procedure codes and costs
- [ ] Each line item explained individually
- [ ] Total cost displayed with breakdown
- [ ] Can save treatment plan for later reference

**Story Points**: 8

---

### F3: Dr. Angel Video Library
**Description**: AI-generated video explanations of common procedures featuring the Dr. Angel avatar.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- [ ] Minimum 25 videos at launch (50 target)
- [ ] 2-3 minutes each
- [ ] Consistent Dr. Angel avatar and voice
- [ ] Searchable by procedure name or code
- [ ] Playback controls (speed, captions)
- [ ] Available offline for premium users

**Story Points**: 21 (content creation heavy)

---

### F4: Family Circle Sharing
**Description**: One-tap sharing of explanations with family members.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- [ ] Generate shareable link or in-app invite
- [ ] Recipient sees clean, formatted explanation
- [ ] No app download required for recipient (web view)
- [ ] Sender can see when recipient viewed
- [ ] Include "Questions to Ask" in shared content

**Story Points**: 8

---

### F5: Questions to Ask Generator
**Description**: Contextual questions patients should ask their dentist based on their specific situation.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- [ ] 5-10 questions generated per procedure
- [ ] Printable/shareable format
- [ ] Explains WHY each question matters
- [ ] Customized based on conversation context

**Story Points**: 5

## 6.3 Data Model Overview

```sql
-- Core Tables

users (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  created_at TIMESTAMP,
  subscription_tier TEXT, -- 'free', 'premium'
  subscription_expires_at TIMESTAMP,
  language_preference TEXT DEFAULT 'en',
  anonymous BOOLEAN DEFAULT true
)

conversations (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  user_id UUID REFERENCES users,
  created_at TIMESTAMP,
  title TEXT, -- auto-generated from first message
  treatment_plan_id UUID -- optional link to uploaded plan
)

messages (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  conversation_id UUID REFERENCES conversations,
  role TEXT, -- 'user' or 'assistant'
  content TEXT,
  created_at TIMESTAMP
)

treatment_plans (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  user_id UUID REFERENCES users,
  image_url TEXT,
  extracted_text TEXT,
  parsed_procedures JSONB, -- structured extraction
  total_cost DECIMAL,
  created_at TIMESTAMP
)

videos (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  procedure_code TEXT, -- ADA code
  procedure_name TEXT,
  video_url TEXT,
  thumbnail_url TEXT,
  duration_seconds INTEGER,
  transcript TEXT,
  language TEXT DEFAULT 'en'
)

shares (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  user_id UUID REFERENCES users,
  conversation_id UUID REFERENCES conversations,
  share_token TEXT UNIQUE, -- for public link
  viewed_at TIMESTAMP,
  created_at TIMESTAMP
)

-- Phase 2 Tables

treatment_buddies (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  user_id UUID REFERENCES users,
  procedure_code TEXT,
  willing_to_connect BOOLEAN,
  outcome_rating INTEGER, -- 1-5 stars
  notes TEXT
)
```

# 10. RISKS & MITIGATIONS

## 10.1 Technical Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| AI gives incorrect information | Medium | High | RAG with verified dental content, human review of edge cases, clear disclaimers |
| OCR fails on poor quality photos | Medium | Medium | Image quality guidance, manual input fallback |
| API costs spike with scale | Medium | Medium | Caching, rate limiting, usage tiers |
| Avatar/voice tech quality issues | Low | Medium | Pre-generate videos, test extensively before launch |

## 10.2 Market Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Big tech enters space (Google Health) | Medium | High | Build community moat, niche focus, brand trust |
| Low willingness to pay | Medium | High | Generous free tier, prove value before paywall |
| Dental board regulatory action | Low | Critical | Education-only positioning, lawyer review, disclaimers |

## 10.3 Execution Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Founder bandwidth (part-time) | Medium | Medium | Automate operations, hire VA, outsource dev |
| Content quality consistency | Medium | Medium | Style guide, prompt engineering, QA process |
| Slow user acquisition | Medium | Medium | Multiple channels, paid ads buffer, iterate on messaging |

## 10.4 Legal/Regulatory Mitigation Strategy

**CRITICAL**: This is the highest-stakes risk.

**Safeguards**:
1. Every screen includes "Educational purposes only" disclaimer
2. AI never says "you should" or "you need" - only "typically" and "commonly"
3. Every response ends with "Discuss with your dentist"
4. No specific diagnosis or treatment recommendations
5. Terms of Service reviewed by healthcare attorney

## 13.2 Dr. Angel Personality Guide

**Voice Characteristics**:
- Warm and friendly, like a trusted uncle/aunt
- Uses simple analogies ("think of a crown like a helmet for your tooth")
- Occasionally uses gentle humor to ease anxiety
- Never condescending, always respectful
- Admits uncertainty when appropriate

**Sample Responses**:

*Question*: "What is a root canal?"

*Dr. Angel*: "Ah, the famous root canal! It's got a scary reputation, but let me tell you what's actually going on. Inside each tooth, there's a soft area called the pulp - think of it as the tooth's nerve center. When that gets infected or damaged, a root canal removes the infected pulp, cleans everything out, and seals it up. The tooth stays in place, just without the nerve inside. Most patients say it's no worse than getting a filling - the scary reputation is mostly outdated! Here are some questions you might want to ask your dentist about your specific situation..."

**Key Phrases**:
- "That's a great question..."
- "Let me explain this in plain terms..."
- "Here's what I'd want to know if I were in your shoes..."
- "Every situation is different, so be sure to discuss with your dentist..."
