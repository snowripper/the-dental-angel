# CRITICAL COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST

> The Dental Angel MUST comply with these - founder's biggest concern.

### Medical/Legal Positioning
- [ ] App clearly states "EDUCATION ONLY"
- [ ] Every screen has appropriate disclaimer
- [ ] AI never says "you should" or "you need"
- [ ] AI always says "typically", "commonly", "often"
- [ ] Every response suggests "discuss with your dentist"
- [ ] No diagnosis or treatment recommendations
- [ ] Terms of Service reviewed by healthcare attorney
- [ ] "This is not dental advice" prominent

### Dr. Angel Safeguards
- [ ] Never tells patients what to do
- [ ] Never says "your dentist is wrong"
- [ ] Always encourages dentist consultation
- [ ] Admits uncertainty when appropriate
- [ ] Frames as education, not advice
