## Final Refined Idea

**The Dental Angel** - *"Understand before you decide"*

A mobile app for patients facing dental procedures over $1,000. Features:
- **Dr. Angel**: AI persona with warm personality, powered by 40 years of real clinical experience
- **AI Chat**: Plain-English explanations of any dental procedure
- **Video Library**: AI-generated avatar explaining 50+ procedures
- **Family Circle**: Instant sharing with loved ones
- **Treatment Buddies**: Connect with others who had same procedure
- **Treatment Comparisons**: Implant vs bridge, etc.

**Revenue**:
| Tier | Price |
|------|-------|
| Free | $0 (3 questions/month) |
| Premium | $9.99/month |
| Practice Partner | $99/month |
| Enterprise | Custom |
| + Affiliate revenue from financing partners |
