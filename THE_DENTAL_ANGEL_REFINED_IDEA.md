## The Full Vision

**The Dental Angel** is a mobile app that transforms how patients understand dental care. Built by a retired UCLA dentist with 40 years of clinical experience (15,000+ real cases), it's the ONLY dental AI trained on actual clinical patterns rather than medical textbooks.

### The Problem (Quantified)

- **47% of patients delay treatment** due to confusion
- Delayed treatment costs **3-5x more** when problems compound
- **$140 billion** dental services market in the US alone
- Average patient understands only **23% of what was explained** (ADA study)
- **150 million** dental visits per year in the US

### The Solution

An AI "dental angel" that speaks like a trusted, experienced dentist friend:
- Explains procedures in plain English
- Provides a "Second Opinion Score" (confidence rating on necessity)
- "Dental Dialect Translator" converts jargon to plain language
- Suggests specific questions to ask YOUR dentist
- Never tells patients what to do - empowers informed decisions

### What Makes This Different (The "ONLY")

**The ONLY dental AI trained on real clinical patterns from 15,000+ actual cases.**

Not Wikipedia. Not medical textbooks. Real clinical experience.
