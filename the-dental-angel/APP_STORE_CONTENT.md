## Full Description

### App Store (iOS) & Google Play

**Tired of dental apps that give you generic advice? So were we.**

You just got a treatment plan from your dentist. Maybe it's a crown, a root canal, or something you've never heard of. You have questions — but the internet gives you scary answers, and your dentist's office is closed.

Other dental apps? They match you with a random dentist who doesn't read your situation. Or a chatbot that says "brush and floss." Or worse — they take your money and tell you to "just see a dentist."

**The Dental Angel is different.**

This app was built by a dentist with 40 years of real experience — someone who actually treated patients one at a time for four decades. Not a script. Not a chatbot menu. A real conversation with an AI dental educator who understands what you're going through.

**Have a Real Conversation**

Ask anything in plain English. Ask follow-up questions. Change the subject. Come back tomorrow and pick up where you left off. The Dental Angel doesn't lose context and never makes you repeat yourself.

- "Why would I need a crown instead of a filling?"
- "Is it normal for this to cost $1,200?"
- "What should I ask my dentist before saying yes?"

You'll get clear, specific, thoughtful answers — not "I don't understand, please select from these options."

**Upload Your Treatment Plan**

Take a photo of your treatment plan or dental paperwork. The Dental Angel will walk through each item, explain what it means, why your dentist may have recommended it, and what questions you should ask before deciding.

**Built for Patients, Not Dentists**

Most dental apps are designed for dental offices. This one is designed for you. Large text, simple navigation, no confusing menus. Just the help you need when you need it.

**40 Years of Real Expertise**

The Dental Angel is powered by the knowledge of a dentist who graduated from UCLA Dental School and spent 40 years in cosmetic, reconstructive, and TMJ dentistry. It speaks like a friendly, experienced dentist who has nothing to sell you — just honest education to help you feel confident about your care.

**Key Features:**

- Chat with an AI dental educator anytime, anywhere
- Upload treatment plans and x-rays for plain-English explanations
- Get specific questions to ask your dentist before your next visit
- Compare typical costs for common procedures
- Decision guides for crowns, root canals, and extractions
- Watch educational videos on 26 dental topics
- Works in any language
- Your conversations stay private on your device
- No subscription required for basic access

**Important:**

The Dental Angel is for education only — it doesn't diagnose, prescribe, or tell you what to do. Only your dentist, who can examine you in person, can give you specific dental advice. We're here to help you understand your options and have better conversations with your dental team.

## Notes for App Store Review

This app provides dental EDUCATION only. It:
- Does NOT diagnose dental conditions
- Does NOT prescribe treatments

- Always encourages users to consult their own dentist
