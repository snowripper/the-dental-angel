# Privacy Policy for The Dental Angel

**Last Updated: January 26, 2026**

## Introduction

The Dental Angel ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Conversation Content**: Questions you ask and information you share about your dental concerns
- **Uploaded Images**: Photos of treatment plans, x-rays, or dental images you choose to upload for analysis

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App features used, session duration
- **Crash Reports**: Technical information to help us fix issues

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide educational responses about dental care
- Analyze uploaded images to help explain dental concepts
- Improve our app and educational content
- Fix technical issues and improve performance

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Your conversation history is stored locally on your device
- We do not maintain copies of your conversations on our servers after processing

### Image Processing
- Images you upload are sent to our AI service provider (OpenAI) for analysis
- Images are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored
- We do not use your images to train AI models

### Security Measures
- All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS
- We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **OpenAI**: For AI-powered educational responses and image analysis
- **Expo/React Native**: For app functionality

These services have their own privacy policies that govern their use of data.

## Data Retention

- **Conversation History**: Stored locally on your device until you delete it
- **Processed Images**: Not retained after analysis is complete
- **Analytics Data**: Retained for up to 12 months in aggregated, anonymized form

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored locally in the app
- **Delete**: Remove your conversation history at any time through the app
- **Opt-Out**: Stop using the app at any time

## Children's Privacy

The Dental Angel is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Medical Disclaimer

The Dental Angel provides educational information only. It is not a substitute for professional dental advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified dental professional for any health concerns.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
