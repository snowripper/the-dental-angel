## About the App

**The Dental Angel** is a mobile app that helps patients understand their dental treatment plans. It's powered by AI that explains procedures in plain language, translates dental jargon, and suggests questions to ask their dentist.

- **100% Educational** вҖ” Not dental advice, not second opinions
- **AI-powered** with Dr. Angel's 40 years of expertise
- **Expert tier** вҖ” Personal educational review from Dr. Angel (available to everyone)
- **Created by** a retired dentist with 40 years of UCLA-trained experience
- **Goal:** Help patients worldwide while generating meaningful income

**Note:** Malpractice insurance for teledentistry second opinions was not obtainable (confirmed Feb 2026). The app remains 100% educational.

---

## Key User Insight

Patients are only interested in their **CURRENT dental problem**. Once their treatment plan is understood and their issue resolved, they have no reason to return вҖ” possibly for years.

**This is a one-time need product**, not a recurring-use app like fitness or language learning.

## The Monetization Model

### Four-Tier Structure

| Tier | Price (USA) | What's Included |
|------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Free Preview** | $0 | Sample conversations, video library, general info вҖ” builds trust but doesn't solve their problem |
| **Quick Answers** | $29 | AI chat for 7 days, treatment plan analysis, plain-language explanations |
| **Full Prep** вӯҗ | $49 | 14 days access + cost comparison + printable questions + summary PDF + red flags checklist |
| **Expert Review** | $149 | Everything above + Dr. Angel's personal educational review |

**All tiers are educational.** Malpractice insurance for teledentistry was not obtainable, so the app does not offer licensed second opinions.

### The Logic Behind This Structure

1. **Free content** gets people in the door and builds trust
2. **The personalized help** for THEIR situation is what they pay for
3. **One-time payment** matches one-time need (no subscription guilt)
4. **$149 anchors the price** вҖ” makes $49 look like the smart choice
5. **Premium tier** provides optional extra income with personal connection
6. **Available worldwide** вҖ” no location restrictions for educational content

## International Pricing Strategy

### The Problem with Flat Global Pricing

Your $29 price feels very different around the world:

| Country | $29 USD Feels Like... |
|---------|----------------------|
| Switzerland | $21 (cheaper) |
| UK/Germany | $29-32 (same) |
| Brazil | $45-55 (expensive) |
| Mexico | $50-60 (expensive) |
| India | $90-120 (very expensive) |

**Apps that charge the same price globally lose up to 85% of potential customers in emerging markets.**

### Why Lower Prices = More Revenue

When Roblox introduced regional pricing:
- **52% more paying customers** in Philippines
- **26% more paying customers** in Brazil
- **Total revenue increased** despite lower prices
